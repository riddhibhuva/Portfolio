    “Happy Hands” – A Smart Dustbin

    groove-machine

    Our hackathon project aims to develop a loop generation tool using advanced pattern recognition from existing audio samples. Through machine learning, the system analyzes rhythmic and melodic structures to identify recurring patterns and variations. Users input samples and receive dynamically generated loops that maintain the essence and style of the originals while offering fresh arrangements. This tool will streamline music creation by providing rapid access to high-quality loops tailored to users' preferences, fostering creativity and productivity in music production.

    Software-Project-Management

    The project is dedicated to a case study analysis titled "Value Results, Not Just Effort" by Venkat Subramaniam, designed for a Software Project Management course. This analysis delves into Subramaniam's insights on the importance of focusing on outcomes rather than merely emphasizing effort in software project management. It explores practical strategies and methodologies to prioritize delivering value to stakeholders over mere activity. Through examining real-world scenarios and Subramaniam's recommendations, the case study aims to provide valuable lessons for project managers in effectively navigating software development projects with a results-oriented mindset.

    Cardinal Health Web Scraping and Inventory Management

    This project focuses on analyzing datasets from multiple timelines/seasons. The dataset can be of MECP or their major competitors in the healthcare industry. For this, integration of web scraping technologies such as Selenium and Beautiful Soup is employed to get the product data and then creating a MySQL database. The different datasets are then analyzed to find out insights about when a certain product is getting out of stock or when the price is increasing/decreasing to enhance business intelligence. Also other than stocking, price increase/decrease, new items or delisting of items, addition/deletion of new medicinal packages are to be analyzed.
